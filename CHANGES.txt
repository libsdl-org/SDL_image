  - IMG_Save()
  - IMG_SaveTyped_IO()
  - IMG_SaveBMP()
  - IMG_SaveBMP_IO()
  - IMG_SaveCUR()
  - IMG_SaveCUR_IO()

  - IMG_SaveICO()
  - IMG_SaveICO_IO()
  - IMG_SaveTGA()
  - IMG_SaveTGA_IO()
  - IMG_SaveWEBP()

* Added functions to load animations:

  - IMG_LoadAPNGAnimation_IO()
  - IMG_LoadAVIFAnimation_IO()
